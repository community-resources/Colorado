## Aggregated Resource Directories
_Search for food, shelter, medical care, job training, and more in one place._

For the most comprehensive support, **check both your specific county AND the statewide resources.**
Local county pages are often superior for finding "immediate-need" resources like **emergency eviction legal aid, local clothing closets, transit vouchers, childcare subsidies, and more.** If a local page is missing a specific category, the statewide search engines below aggregate thousands of **broader assistance resources** that cover the entire state.

### Statewide Resource Directories
Large-scale databases covering the entire state of Colorado.

May contain support not listed in the county directories.
- **[2-1-1 Colorado](https://www.211colorado.org/)**  The primary search engine for all Colorado human services. Search by zip code or category. Multilingual, 24/7.
	- **Phone:** Dial `2-1-1` 
	- **Text:** Your Zip Code to `898-211`
- **[FindHelp.org](https://www.findhelp.org)** Search for free or reduced-cost services like medical care, food, housing, and more by zip code.
